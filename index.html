Type, Copy, Paste...easy

Simply type in your caption/comment below just as you'd like it to be seen (i.e. with line breaks and all), then hit "Convert & Copy to Clipboard" and paste it into your Instagram caption/comment. It will be formatted just as you'd like.


Save this bad boy for later

Want to save this little guy for easy access next time? It's super easy, read on to see how. (This is shown using an iPhone but the process is very similar for Android.)

Step 1

Tap the share button.

Step 2

Tap "Add to Home Screen".

Step 3

Give it a name, something like "Insta-caption". Then tap "Add" to add it to your home screen.

Voila!

Now it's available for easy access next time. Easy, right? Feel free to move the icon around just as you would a normal app. Perhaps right next to Instagram?
